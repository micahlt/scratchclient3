We're proud to announce the 0.6 Beta of Scratch Client 3!


Introducing version 0.6.1, the first stable version of the Scratch Client 3 project! Here’s how it works: visit https://micahlt.github.io/scratchclient3/?x=griffpatch and replace “griffpatch” with your own username! Currently, you’ll be able to see the user’s name, profile picture, and their most recent projects. Bright colors hurting your eyes? Click the dark mode button in the navbar.
